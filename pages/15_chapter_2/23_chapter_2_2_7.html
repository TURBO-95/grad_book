The Generation Phase: Prompt Engineering for Grounding

The final phase of the RAG architecture synthesizes the retrieved information. The IntgraServeAI orchestrator (utilizing frameworks like LangChain) dynamically constructs an augmented prompt. This prompt is carefully engineered to include strict system instructions, the retrieved top-\(k\) text chunks, and the user's original query. The LLM acts purely as a highly advanced reasoning and summarization engine, translating the raw, retrieved corporate data into a natural, conversational response for the end-user.
